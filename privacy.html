# Privacy Policy for Rocksmith Request Twitch Extension **Last updated:** July 2026 This privacy policy applies to the Rocksmith Request Twitch Extension (“Extension”) developed by Eatcaviar. The Extension is designed to display a list of codes that viewers can request via chat commands (e.g., `!play CODE`). It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. ## Information We Do Not Collect - We do not collect any personally identifiable information (e.g., names, email addresses, IP addresses, or Twitch user IDs). - We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools. - We do not access or store any data from your Twitch account, device, or browser. ## Third-Party Services The Extension loads the list from a remote JSON URL (configured by the broadcaster). This data is publicly accessible and contains only challenges titles and codes. No personal data is involved. ## Data Sharing We do not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties because we collect none. ## User Controls Viewers can request challenges by typing commands in chat. The Extension only displays the requested code locally; it does not log or transmit these requests. ## Children’s Privacy The Extension does not target children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from minors. ## Changes to This Policy Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. ## Contact If you have questions about this policy, please contact: ericfloaters@gmail.com
