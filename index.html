Privacy Policy for Rocksmith Request Twitch Extension

Last updated: July 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Rocksmith Request Twitch Extension (“Extension”) developed by Eatcaviar. The Extension is designed to display a list of codes that viewers can request via chat commands (e.g., !play CODE). It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users except for the optional contact form provided for reporting issues or sending feedback.

Information We Collect

1. When using the Extension (passive usage)

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information (e.g., names, email addresses, IP addresses, or Twitch user IDs).
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools.
  • We do not access or store any data from your Twitch account, device, or browser.

2. When you voluntarily submit the contact form

If you choose to use the Report an issue page (hosted on GitHub Pages), you may provide:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address (optional – to allow us to reply)
  • Your message (required)
  • The type of issue (selected from a dropdown)

This information is sent only to our email address via the third‑party service Formspree (see below). We use this data solely to respond to your inquiry or to improve the Extension. We do not store this data in any database, and we do notshare it with anyone other than Formspree for the purpose of delivery.

Third‑Party Services

Formspree

The contact form on our report page uses Formspree to forward your message to our email inbox. Formspree acts as a data processor. When you submit the form, your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree’s servers and then emailed to us. Formspree’s own privacy policy can be found at https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/.

Remote List

The Extension loads the list from a remote JSON URL (configured by the broadcaster). This data is publicly accessible and contains only challenge titles and codes. No personal data is involved.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information. We share minimal data (name, email, message) only with Formspree for the sole purpose of delivering your message to us.

Data Retention

  • Emails received via the contact form are kept in our email inbox. We may retain them for as long as needed to address your issue, and we will delete them upon your request.
  • We do not create any user profiles or databases.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any data we hold about you (we hold none except your email correspondence).
  • Request deletion of your data (we will delete your email).

User Controls

Viewers can request challenges by typing commands in chat. The Extension only displays the requested code locally; it does not log or transmit these requests.

Children’s Privacy

The Extension does not target children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from minors. The contact form is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
